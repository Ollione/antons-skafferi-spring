Detailed Description

APIController

This class is the controller for the API. It handles all the requests and responses for the API. It is responsible for the communication between the frontend and the backend.

The

APIController

class provides endpoints for managing

Bookings

,

Dinner

,

DrinkOrder

,

Drinks

,

Employee

,

Events

,

FoodOrder

,

Items

,

Lunch

,

LunchItems

,

Orders

,

Person

,

Role

,

Shift

,

Tab

,

Tables

and

WorksShift

.

Each endpoint is mapped to a specific URL path and HTTP method.

The class uses the DatabaseService to perform CRUD operations on the database.

See also
DatabaseService
